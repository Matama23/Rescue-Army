About us

At the Rescue Army, we strive to make ‘homelessness’ a challenge of the past.

Our mission is to provide a beacon of hope for individuals currently experiencing homelessness.By offering a variety of comprehensive support settings to assist persons disproportionately affected by homelessness, we can support marganlised communities and increase access to equitable health outcomes for all.

Rescue Army aims to further empower persons affected by homelessness, through a well-researched range of social, emotional, spiritual, and social support. Our dedication can provide persons affected by homelessness the opportunity to participate in their communities with justice and dignity, with further options for financial and housing support.Our vision is to create a society where everyone has an equal chance.

We pride ourselves in offering multifaceted support outlets, empowering people in an inclusive and respectful manner, regardless of their individual circumstances. We believe that by addressing both the immediate and long-term needs faced by persons affected by homelessness, we can help build an equitable community, allowing all people to reach their full potential.

Long term, we aim to build a future where homelessness is completely eradicated, and every person has access to the available resources and opportunities to reach their full potential. One step at a time, Rescue Army seeks to create a brighter, more inclusive world, where no one is left behind.

Our Mission

The Rescue Army brings hope to individuals encountering injustice and challenges by providing social, spiritual and financial support. The purpose of our campaign is to raise awareness of the problems that homeless people experience in everyday life through storytelling, highlighting the unique challenges that homelessness community face In Australia. Through this we aim to establish a positive community where individuals are free from the burdens of injustice and where everyone has the opportunity to prosper.

  • Emergency shelter
  • Food and nutrition assistance
  • Job training and placement
  • Healthcare access and support
  • Community resources and referrals

Our Vision

We envision a world where everyone has access to safe housing, basic needs, and opportunities for personal growth. Together, we can create lasting change and support those in need.
